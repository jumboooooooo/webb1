Kang Seul-gi (Korean: 강슬기; born February 10, 1994),

known mononymously as Seulgi, is a South Korean singer and dancer.

She is a member of girl group Red Velvet, its sub-unit Red Velvet – Irene & Seulgi, and the supergroup Got the Beat. She released her debut EP, 28 Reasons, in 2022.
